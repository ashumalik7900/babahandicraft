Our Story & Heritage

Directly from the Brass City of India—Moradabad. We manufacture premium metalware, silver platters, and designer brass decor with unmatched quality.

Legacy of Moradabad Artisans

Moradabad ko poori duniya me "Pital Nagri" (Brass City) ke naam se jaana jata hai. BaBa Handicrafts me hum isi mitti ki sadiyon puraani art ko zinda rakh rahe hain. Hamare saare products hand-carved, sand-casted aur traditional chemical plating se taiyar hote hain jo unhe ek royal, long-lasting finish dete hain.

Hum bade-bade exporters, gift suppliers, hotels, aur retailers ko direct factory pricing par bulk supply dete hain, jisse beech ka middlemen commission 100% khatam ho jata hai.
