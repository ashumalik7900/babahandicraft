Wholesale Terms & Shipping

Kripya order submit karne se pehle hamari B2B commercial transport aur order policies ko dhyan se padh lein.

📦 1. Minimum Order Value (MOV)

Hamara business pure wholesale (B2B) hai, isliye hum single ya retail pieces me deal nahi karte. Website par order submit karne ke liye aapke cart ka total subtotal kam se kam ₹5,000 hona zaroori hai. Isse kam ka order process nahi kiya jayega.

🚚 2. Transport & Shipping (To-Pay Basis)

₹9,999 se niche ke orders par ₹350 transport shipping charge lagta hai. ₹9,999 se upar ke orders par shipping portal par free dikhayega, par dhyan dein ki heavy commercial bulk transport ka bhada (freight charges) hamesha 'To-Pay' basis par hoga. Yani jab transport aapke shahar me maal deliver karega, tab aapko transport office me bhada dena hoga.

⚡ 3. Manufacturing & Dispatch Time

Wholesale orders ko secure pack karne me samay lagta hai. Aapka order Proforma Invoice clear hone ke baad 3 se 5 working days ke andar Moradabad factory se trusted transport (jaise V-Xpress, TCI Freight, ARC) par dispatch kar diya jayega. Gadi par maal chadhte hi aapko Bilty/LR copy WhatsApp par bhej di jayegi.

🛡️ 4. Transit Breakage & Safe Packing

Handicrafts items naazuk hote hain, isliye hum heavy-duty 5-layer bubble wrap aur wooden box packaging use karte hain. Agar transit me koi product major damage hota hai, toh unboxing ka bina-cut kiya hua video zaroori hai. Sahi proof hone par hum agle order me uski value adjust kar dete hain.

📄 5. GST Invoice & ITC Claim

Hum proper B2B GST tax invoice provide karte hain. Agar aap dukan/company ke naam par Input Tax Credit (ITC) claim karna chahte hain, toh order karte waqt apna sahi GSTIN number zaroor enter karein.
